Meet Anandi Ramanathan

Blending watercolor dreams with mindfulness and joy.

Anandi Ramanathan is a watercolor and acrylic artist inspired by nature and her travels. She loves to observe, admire, and capture the beauty of the world through her art. Specializing in florals and illustrations, her paintings reflect a sense of joy and bliss that she hopes to share with others.

With over four years of experience, Anandi also conducts workshops to share her journey with watercolors and the knowledge she has gained along the way.
